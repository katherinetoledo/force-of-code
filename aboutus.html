We are a group of unique women, entirely different from each other. We only had one thing in common.. we all went to the twitter HQ every Wednesday evening for 6 weeks, to learn code! We probably would never have met each other, if we hadn't decided to do this course and now we each have 3 new friends! It just goes to show, yet again, how the wonder of technology is helping bring real life connections together. We wanted to create this site so that, every fantastic lady that joins CFG, has a chance to share a little about their journey with coding and eventually this site can hopefully become a platform for women wanting to somehow enter the world of tech (LinkedIn for Ladies) !
